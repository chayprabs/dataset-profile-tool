## Important notice

## Scope

Applies to the Operator's **hosted** Service. Self-hosted installs are controlled by you unless you adopt this policy.

## Information we process

- Files, URLs, and derived profiles, schemas, drift reports, samples, warnings
- Share-link snapshots until TTL expiry
- Technical logs (IP, User-Agent, timestamps) for operations and security
- No sign-in required for basic use; no intentional collection of payment cards

## What we do not do

- Sell personal information
- Use uploads to train third-party ad/ML models
- Embed ad/analytics trackers in the distributed app codebase

## Legal bases (EEA/UK)
